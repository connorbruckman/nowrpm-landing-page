Your health, made simple.

NowRPM is a health and wellness program, engineered for the modern era. Stay in constant communication with your care providers, have access to high-fidelity, real-time health data, and reduce readmissions. All devices are at no cost to Medicare patients, and come ready to use out of the box -- no setup, smartphone, apps, or WiFi required.

What is NowRPM?

NowRPM enables continuous patient monitoring, breaking the episodic care paradigm. Receive a full suite of smart devices, designed to help you achieve your health goals.

Receive 24/7, one-on-one consultation from trained medical professionals.
Get automated medication reminders by phone and text.
Totally free shipping in the contiguous United States.

What comes in the box?

Samsung Tablet:
The base of operations for every NowRPM kit. See your past readings, and trigger new ones.


Canny Smart Scale:
A Bluetooth-enabled weight scale, with a sleek glass finish. Track your weight, BMI, bone density and more.


E86 Smart Watch:
Track your blood pressure, heart rate, ECG/EKG, sleep patterns, and blood oxygen in style.


True Metrix Glucometer:
Qualifying diabetic and pre-diabetic patients can receive a smart blood glucose meter, in addition to the other devices.
